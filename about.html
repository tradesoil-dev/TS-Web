About Tradesoil

A lean consultancy built for global trade.

Tradesoil International is a global trade consulting and sourcing company headquartered in Sri Lanka. We specialise in oleochemicals, biodiesel feedstocks, and specialty raw materials, connecting buyers and suppliers across Asia, Europe, and the Middle East.

Consultancy model

We're not a trading house. We add intelligence, strategy, and execution, not just a connection between buyer and seller.

Asia network depth

Deep, trusted supplier relationships across Malaysia, Indonesia, India, and China, built through years of direct engagement.

AI-augmented

AI-integrated model for faster RFQ processing, smarter pricing intelligence, and better sourcing outcomes for every client.

Leadership
GG

Gladwin Gerald

Founder, Tradesoil International

Gladwin is a global trade entrepreneur driven by a simple belief: meaningful business is built on trust, transparency, and long-term relationships.

Over the past decade, he has worked across international raw material and commodity markets, developing supplier networks throughout Asia and supporting customers across Europe, the Middle East, and beyond. Through Tradesoil International, he helps businesses identify reliable sourcing opportunities, navigate market complexities, and make better commercial decisions through practical market intelligence.

His vision is to transform Tradesoil from a traditional sourcing business into a technology enabled commodity intelligence platform that creates value for buyers and suppliers worldwide.

ED

Eugene Delain

Co-Founder / CTO, Tradesoil International

Eugene combines deep technology expertise with practical experience in global trade finance. Having worked within the trade finance operations of a leading international bank, he developed a strong understanding of cross-border transactions, trade documentation, compliance processes, and the financial mechanisms that support international commerce. As Co-Founder and CTO of Tradesoil International, he leverages this unique blend of financial and technical knowledge to drive digital innovation, strengthen operational efficiency, and support the company's mission of building trusted trade partnerships across global markets.
